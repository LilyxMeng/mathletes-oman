Course Structure

  • Each class lasts 1 hour
  • Classes are conducted twice a week
  • Each course lasts 1 month, which means students will have 8 lessons
  • In these classes, students will be taught core topics, provided with personalised resources, and have the support and guidance of our tutors
  • Classes are conducted on Google Classroom
  • Upon registration, we will send you an invite to our classroom
  • Through this platform, we will be providing our students personalised resources and assignments
