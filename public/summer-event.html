Mathletes Summer Competition

What is it?

Welcome to Oman’s first ever student-run national competition for middle-school students across the country! This is your one stop station to achieve greatness and compete with the greatest minds of the nation. Students will engage in a multitude of activities which will test their problem solving and logical skills. At the end of the event, the team with the highest points will be crowned national champions!

What are the Events?

Mathletes Challenge

One answer, one reward. The challenge pushes students beyond their school curriculum, emphasising on their ability to problem solve under pressure. Apply your knowledge to mind teasing problems within an hour! Circle the correct option and you are one step closer to the ultimate prize.

Mathletes Bowl

The battle of the greatest team in the room! Clash against other teams in a mega virtual bowl. Work collaboratively to solve unusual questions in different contexts. Don’t be surprised if your question links to a Netflix show! Fastest finger (or shall we say fingers) wins!

Mathletes Escape Room

Mysterious. Puzzling. Thrilling. Put on your detective hats! Work together to find clues from each other’s screens to piece together a solution. Team with the quickest time to escape the (virtual) room wins!
